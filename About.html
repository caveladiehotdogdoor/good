About Us

Our business group is called Flora Jewels. We chose the name Flora Jewels because our products are made out of flowers and we thought the name represented us well.



Our products are dried flowers set in resin to make a pendant and then attached to a necklace chain or earring hooks.



Flora Jewels aims to help people feel good about themselves to feel good about themselves while wearing our product.



Our jewellery is sold at $4.50 for one necklace or one pair of earrings.



We sell two types of necklace, either with a silver or gold chain. Our earrings are a smaller variety as we only offer silver earrings.



Flora jewels market will be hold at break 1 on the 2nd of August in the Red Square.
