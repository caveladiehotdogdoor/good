OUR MEMBERS/ HISTORY






















As a year 11 group, we researched jewellery and wanted to create unique jewellery using flowers. We decided to use resources that most of the group members already had at home. This is how we chose to use the flower presses we already had at home to create pressed flower earrings and necklaces.
